Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

LeapNest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Apple Sign-In Information

If you choose to create an account or sign in using Apple Sign-In, Apple may provide us with the following information associated with your Apple ID, subject to your privacy settings and Apple's terms:

  • Your name (first and last name, if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (if you choose to share it, or a private relay email if you use Hide My Email)

You can control what information Apple shares with us through your Apple ID privacy settings. You can also revoke our access to your Apple ID at any time through your Apple account settings. If you revoke access, you may need to create a new account using email and password to continue using LeapNest. For more information about how Apple handles your data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Financial Information

To help you track your home buying journey, we collect:

  • Income sources and amounts
  • Monthly expenses by category
  • Property information (current and dream home)
  • Savings goals and progress

Subscription and Payment Information

If you choose to subscribe to Premium or Ultimate tiers, we collect:

  • Subscription tier selection (Premium or Ultimate)
  • Billing period preference (monthly or yearly)
  • Payment information (processed through platform payment providers)
  • Subscription status and billing history

Payment information is processed securely through platform payment providers (Apple App Store for iOS). We do not store your full payment card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by Apple's secure payment system.

Usage Information

We may automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our app, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent)
  • Error logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Calculate your home buying readiness and savings progress
  • Send you notifications about milestones and reminders (if enabled)
  • Provide personalized recommendations (if enabled)
  • Respond to your requests and support inquiries
  • Protect against fraud and enhance security
  • Improve our app through analytics (if you opt-in)

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our app (e.g., Supabase for database hosting)
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transaction (merger, sale, etc.)
  • With your explicit consent

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Google Places API: Address autocomplete functionality
  • Apple App Store: Subscription billing and payment processing (iOS)
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication service (iOS) - When you use Apple Sign-In, your authentication is handled by Apple, and Apple may share certain information with us as described in the "Apple Sign-In Information" section above

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them, particularly Apple's Privacy Policy if you use Apple Sign-In.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure authentication mechanisms

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Export your data (available in Settings)
  • Delete your data (available in Settings)
  • Opt-out of notifications and personalized features
  • Request deletion of your account

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@leapnest.app

10. Subscription Tiers and Data Access

LeapNest offers three subscription tiers:

  • Free Tier: Limited to 2 properties (oldest active and oldest prospect property)
  • Premium Tier: Up to 5 properties with workflow features (prospect → in-progress → active)
  • Ultimate Tier: Unlimited properties with all premium features and priority support

Your subscription tier determines which features and how many properties you can access. If your subscription expires or is cancelled, you will retain access to your data but may be limited to Free tier features. All your property data is preserved regardless of subscription status.

11. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Subscription and billing information may be retained for legal and accounting purposes even after account deletion. You can request deletion of your data at any time through the app settings.

12. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.
